I am interested in HCI, with a focus on AR/VR and AI programming. I enjoy creating software that combines these technologies, such as educational programs or control systems that support collaboration.

I am also interested in using AI models like GPT and reinforcement learning to build human-AI interactions in virtual environments or user interfaces. Rather than focusing on technical engineering or optimization, I aim to explore new ideas for using AI and computer interaction to enhance education, productivity, and gaming in a human-centered way.

My background includes research and development in AR/VR using Unity during both my undergraduate and graduate studies. During my undergraduate years, I made on creating emotional interactions with AI-driven NPCs in an AR environment. In my master’s, I researched reducing motion sickness in VR using sound, specifically studying whether reverse sound stimuli can help reduce motion sickness.

I also have experience building various applications using Python, such as web applications with Flask and FastAPI, desktop software, automation scripts, chatbots, and trading systems. And I have an experience of publishing mobile games ( 5 more ) and VR Game. I am using Unity Engine for making games. Additionally, I have used JavaScript to develop APIs and automation servers, studied smart contracts with Solidity for blockchain applications, and created over 50 trading indicators using Pinescript.
